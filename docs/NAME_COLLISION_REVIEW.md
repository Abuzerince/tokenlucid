# TokenLucid / TLCD preliminary collision review

Date: 2 July 2026  
Status: preliminary only - not a trademark clearance or legal opinion

## Findings

- Exact-name web searches for `TokenLucid` did not identify an indexed crypto project or software product using the same combined name.
- Searches for `TLCD` did not identify a clearly indexed CoinGecko or CoinMarketCap crypto asset using that exact ticker.
- `TLCD` is widely used outside crypto as an abbreviation for "tuned liquid column damper" and in unrelated technical/academic contexts. A ticker is not exclusive ownership.
- The broader word `Lucid` is crowded, including existing crypto and non-crypto products. Branding must consistently use the full `TokenLucid` name and the mint address, not `Lucid` alone.

## Required before mainnet

1. Run exact and similarity searches in EUIPO TMview, WIPO Global Brand Database and the relevant national registry for the chosen legal entity and target markets.
2. Search relevant Nice classes for software, financial technology, downloadable software, SaaS and education/research services.
3. Have qualified trademark counsel review confusing similarity, owner identity and filing strategy.
4. Re-check CoinGecko, CoinMarketCap, major explorers, GitHub, npm, app stores and social platforms immediately before public launch.
5. Do not mark `trademarkSearchCompleted` as true until the formal review is documented.
