Responsible disclosure

The production security address will be activated on the official domain before public launch. Never send seed phrases, private keys or personal financial information.

Scope

The TokenLucid website, API and published token deployment tooling are in scope. Third-party wallets, RPC providers, Solana programs and social-engineering reports are outside our control but may be forwarded to the relevant provider.

Report content

Include the affected URL or file, reproducible steps, impact and a safe proof of concept. Do not access other users' data, disrupt service, demand payment, or publish an unresolved vulnerability.
